Service Station Attendant-Full TIme at Sitka Petro Express
Petro Marine Services - Sitka, AK
Petro Express, Sitka has an opening for a regular full-time employee to assist customers at the service station. This is a year-round position that also offers a generous benefit package!

Apply online at

www.petromarineservices.com

. Petro 49, Inc. promotes a smoke-free and drug-free workplace - must be able to pass a post-offer, pre-employment drug test.

DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

Assist customers at the station by pumping fuel, cleaning windows, checking fluid levels and tire pressure, receiving payment and giving change.

Practice safe work procedures for your safety and the safety of others, including wearing personal protective equipment as required.

Keep customer service areas in clean and safe condition.

Perform routine maintenance on dispensers, car wash, etc.

Fill customer propane tanks as requested. (Minimum age of 18 to handle propane).

Maintain inventory levels of products in station.

Other general station duties as needed.

Requirements

No experience required, on-the-job training is available. General understanding of how automobiles work, and some sales ability.

Must be able to work evenings and weekends.

Attendant will spend most of the time working outdoors in all kinds of weather and may work inside part of the time. Odors, vibrations, and mechanical hazards are common. Possible injuries include cuts and burns from hot engines.

Professional and courteous customer service skills; work as part of a team and be able to accept responsibility. Work overtime during busy season; handle stressful situations and multi-task. Must be able to lift at least 50 lbs. and be on your feet for long periods of time. Must be able to pass a pre-employment drug test.

WORK ENVIRONMENT

Petro 49, Inc. promotes affirmative action for minorities, women, disabled persons, and veterans. Petro 49, Inc. promotes a smoke-free workplace and as such, limits smoking at all facilities and is a drug-free workplace.
